1. Open Firefox.
2. Go to this url "about:debugging".
3. Click "Load temporary addon" button.
4. Choose "manifest.json" from the zip.
5. Now, the addon icon must appear in the toolbar. Try it.
6. Open Console window by pressing "Ctr' + Shift + I".
7. Copy the log data from Console tab.
8. Save it to a text file, and send it to the developer.
